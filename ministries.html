A Closer Look

Prayer Ministry

A House of Prayer for All Nations

Our prayer ministry is the engine room of Mission Church Africa. Before every service, our intercessors gather to prepare the atmosphere. We believe nothing significant happens without prayer first.

  • Weekly corporate prayer every Tuesday 6–8 PM
  • 24-hour prayer chain rotations during special seasons
  • Prayer counsellors available after every service
  • Annual three-day prayer & fasting conference
PK
Ministry Leader Pastor Kenneth
Outreach & Missions

Taking the Gospel to Every Corner

From Kampala's streets to rural villages in Uganda and beyond — our outreach teams go where the need is greatest. We partner with local leaders and international networks to see communities transformed.

  • Monthly community outreach in Kampala slums
  • Prison ministry visiting correctional facilities
  • School evangelism and discipleship programmes
  • Hospital visitation and healing ministry
BD
Ministry Leader Bishop David Ssekamatte
