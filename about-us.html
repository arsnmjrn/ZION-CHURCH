Our Story,
Mission & Faith

Why We Exist

Built on Purpose

At Zion, everything we do is anchored in the Word of God and driven by love for people.

Our Mission

To preach the Gospel of Jesus Christ with power, make disciples of all nations, and raise a generation of Spirit-filled believers who impact their communities for God's Kingdom.

Our Vision

To be a global centre of revival, healing, and transformation — a church known for the undeniable presence of God, where every person discovers their divine purpose.

Our Foundation

We believe in the full authority of Scripture, the power of the Holy Spirit, the Lordship of Jesus Christ, and the great commission to reach every soul with the saving grace of God.

2006 Founded
Our History

How It All Began

Zion International Pentecostal Church was born from a prayer meeting, a burning desire, and an unshakeable faith that God was calling a people to His purposes in Kampala.

2006 — The Beginning

A small prayer group gathered in Makindye West with a vision to plant a church that would transform the community and beyond.

2010 — First Sanctuary

God provided the resources to build our first permanent sanctuary. The congregation grew rapidly as signs and wonders followed the ministry.

2015 — Global Reach

With members now in multiple countries, ZIPC established international connections and began streaming services online, reaching thousands globally.

2026 — Today

A thriving congregation of over 500 members, active across four ministries, continuing to carry the fire of revival throughout Uganda and beyond.

Core Beliefs

What We Stand For

01

The Word of God

We hold the Bible as the inspired, infallible Word of God — the supreme authority for all matters of faith, conduct, and eternal life.

02

Spirit-Filled Worship

We believe in authentic, fervent worship that ushers in the presence of God and transforms lives from the inside out.

03

Community & Discipleship

We are committed to raising mature disciples of Christ through intentional fellowship, mentorship, and small group communities.

04

Prayer & Intercession

Prayer is the heartbeat of our church. We are a house of prayer that believes in the miraculous power of God to heal, restore, and deliver.

05

Evangelism & Missions

We carry a global mandate to preach the Gospel to every nation, every tribe, and every person — because every soul matters to God.

06

Generosity & Giving

We believe in a culture of generosity — giving freely of our time, talent, and treasure to advance God's Kingdom and serve one another.
